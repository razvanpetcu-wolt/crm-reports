Executive summary
FIFA — short-term cannibalisation
Both FIFA campaigns show negative GOV at post-7 days (ALB -€2,882, XKX -€12,777). This is a typical cannibalisation pattern — CRM reaches users who would have ordered anyway, temporarily suppressing organic behaviour.
FIFA — long-term value confirmed
Both recover positively at post-28 days (ALB +€1,987, XKX +€7,004), confirming the campaigns build genuine long-term value beyond the initial displacement window.
XKX FIFA outperforms ALB FIFA
XKX post-28 day GOV (+€7,004) is 3.5× higher than ALB (+€1,987) despite a smaller exposed audience (133K vs 192K). XKX shows a stronger incremental response per user.
Gjilan — city launch playbook works
Still generating +€516 incremental GOV at post-28 days from just 1,261 exposed users. A strong efficiency result and a clear case for applying this playbook to future city launches.
