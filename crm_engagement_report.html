Executive summary
ALB email — strong and growing
Volume grew to 104K by July with notable spikes at Jun 8 (97K) and Jun 29 (97K). Open rates holding at 44–60%. The most reliable and scalable channel in the market.
XKX email — volume recovering
Volume peaked at 39K in June after a slow April start. Open rates (25–47%) are lower than ALB but showing improvement into July.
ALB push — high reach and engagement
Delivered 154K at peak with open rates ranging 0.33–2.64%. Strong spikes in April and July signal high receptivity around campaign moments. Consistently outperforms XKX push.
XKX push — reach without engagement
Despite reaching 96K at peak, push open rates stay mostly below 1%. Volume is growing but click-through optimisation is needed.
XKX unsub — improving but monitor
Started April at 0.6% — above the 0.2% threshold. Declining to ~0.1% by July is a positive trend. Audience health is improving but requires ongoing monitoring.
CTR gap — both markets
Email CTR stays below 1% in both markets despite strong open rates. Content is generating interest but not converting to clicks. A CTA and creative review is recommended.
